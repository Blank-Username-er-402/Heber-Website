Activites and leisure

Swimming at the crater

Miday's unique geography and location allow it to have
thermal hot springs. One of these said hot spring has
been developed into a swimming/diving location. The crater
provides relaxing warm water swimming as well as deeper
water scuba diving. Come check out this unique thermal
location today!

Skiing at Soldier Hollow

Soldier Hollow provides world class nordic skiing even
having hosted IBU world cups for both nordic skiing and
biathlon. Soldier Hollow even hosted the 2002 Olympics for
nordic skiing and biathlon. You can take group and private
lessons as well as testing out biathlon with the biathlon experience.

Mountain Biking The WOW

Heber and Midway also provide great mountain biking trails.
One of these trails is the the Wasatch Over Wasatch
or WOW trail. This trail stretches for about 20 miles and
provides great uphill difficulty with challenging but not
expert downhills.
