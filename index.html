The cities of Heber and Midway are two beautiful cties nestled within wasatch county. They offer close proximity
to ski resorts as well as providing many great summer activities such as mountain biking and trailrunning. In the
winter it is generally very cold with a lot of snow and in the summer it is very hot and dry. Other leisure activities
include fishing, camping, and hiking in the wilderness that Heber and midway provide access to.
