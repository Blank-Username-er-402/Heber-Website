Shops and Retail

Fish Heads Fly Shop

Come visit fish heads fly shop for all you flyfishing
equipment. As heber is located close to many rivers
and lakes, fishing and specifically Flyfising are
popular pastimes. At Fish Heads you can get all your
fly fishing equipment from a local business.

Driving through the Uintas

The Uintas are a stunning mountain range a short drive
away from heber. While most people prefer to take a scenic
drive there is a lot of camping and hiking trails as well.
If you want to expereience high mountain scenery theres
nowhere else quite like the Uintas.

Visiting Huber Grove

Huber grove is an old apple orchard in Midway near the
Guardsmans pass road. This venue offers relaxed walks near
the golf course as well as great scenery in the fall. For relaxed
walks and good scenery away from the hustle and bustle of
the world come here!
