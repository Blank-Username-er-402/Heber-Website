Shops and Retail

Fish Heads Fly Shop

Come visit fish heads fly shop for all you flyfishing
equipment. As heber is located close to many rivers
and lakes, fishing and specifically Flyfising are
popular pastimes. At Fish Heads you can get all your
fly fishing equipment from a local business.

Dining at the Midway Mercantile

The midway mercantile is a unique and high quality
restaurant tucked in midways beatiful scenery. Come here
to enjoy a high quality meal while talking in their beatifull
furnished dining spaces.

Timber Knives

If you want to see some of the nicest handcrafted knives
come here! Timber Knives offers high quality handcrafted knives,
hatchets, axes, and much more. Come here to experince both art
and buy yourself a trusty pocketknife.
